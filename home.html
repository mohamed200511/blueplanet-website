Understanding the Ocean Pollution Crisis and Its Global Impact

Oceans cover over 70% of Earth and provide oxygen, food, and habitats. Pollution from plastics, chemicals, and waste threatens marine life and the health of our planet.
Millions of tons of waste enter oceans yearly, disrupting ecosystems and contaminating seafood consumed by humans worldwide.
This website shows causes, impacts, and solutions to pollution.

Plastics on Oceans

12 million tons of plastic enter the oceans every year.

Marine Lifes

100,000 marine mammals die annually from entanglement or ingestion.

Coral Reefs

50% of coral reefs have been damaged by pollution.

Ocean Acidity

30% increase in ocean acidity since the Industrial Revolution.
